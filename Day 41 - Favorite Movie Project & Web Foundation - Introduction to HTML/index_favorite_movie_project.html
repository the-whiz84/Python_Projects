Radu's Greatest Movies of All Time

Top 10 best movies I've watched

The Shawshank Redemption

This is the only movie I can gladly re-watch anytime.
One of the classic movies that everyone should watch at least once.

The Matrix

One of the best sci-fi movies ever made. The first is still the best of the quadrilogy.

Blade Runner

A cult classic and the movie that made cyberpunk what it is now.
It inspired countless themes in movies, tv and games.

Alien

For fans of sci-fi and/or horror movies, this is a must see.
The sequel, Aliens, is also pretty good.

Terminator 2

Although this is the second in the franchise, the first one does not hold up as good as T2.

Jurassic Park

Although there are many sequels, the original one is still, in my opinion, the greatest dinosaur movie.

Predator

Together with Alien, they created an entire universe and multiple franchises and crossovers.
After Terminator 2, this is Arnold Schwarzenegger's best role.

The Lord of the Rings trilogy

I can't say that one of them is better than the others, the entire trilogy is a must-see.
I recommend you get the extended editions and watch all 3 of them.

Interstellar

One of the most thought provoking sci-fi movies ever.

The Dark Knight

If you haven't seen this movie, wth are you doing with your free time?!

Bonus:

- first 6 movies from Star Wars - Skywalker Saga
- Rogue One - a Star Wars story
- The Notebook - for incurable romantics
- The Expanse series - most scientifically accurate rendition of space travel ever.
